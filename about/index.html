Past highlights

Picture runway lights and passport stamps. Agencies, crowns, and covers. Then a twist that feels like a streaming thriller. A private island. A kidnapping. An escape. Later, a different kind of fight. Identity theft, a SIM swap, and blackmail attempts. I reported the identity theft to the FBI and the case led to prosecution. Those seasons are over. I kept the poise, boundaries, and empathy. Now it is computers, hikes, reading, writing, and family. I use that story to help other people protect their own.
