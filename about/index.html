Crazy facts about me

  • The island story. There was a private island, a kidnapping, and an escape. I do not glamorize it. Survival taught me calm and courage that I use every day.
  • Crowns and covers. I won pageants and landed magazine covers. It gave me poise, presence, and a sense for the tiny details that make a story land.
  • Identity theft, reported, prosecuted. I filed with the FBI and the case led to prosecution. If you are in that mess, you are not alone.
  • SIM swapped and blackmailed. I rebuilt my security from the ground up. Keys, backups, and quiet routines for the win.
  • Open internet homebody. Self hosting, clean domains, private chat, and platforms that put people first are my happy place.
  • Soft heart, real toolkit. I read, hike, write, and help people protect what matters. That includes memories, names, and quiet.

Account and identity safety, passkeys and MFA, key management and YubiKey, PGP basics, data broker removals, device hardening, phishing drills, travel security, doxxing and impersonation response, incident cleanup, and reputation rebuild plans.
